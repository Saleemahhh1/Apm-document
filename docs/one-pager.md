# What is Agro Predict?

A decentralized prediction market for forecasting agricultural events:
- Commodity prices  
- Weather outcomes  
- Crop yields  
- Pest outbreaks  

Users stake **APM tokens** and earn rewards for accurate predictions.

---

- Farmers rely on guesswork

- Commodity prices unpredictable  
- No incentives for accurate data  
- Centralized platforms lack transparency
