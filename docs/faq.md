## 1. What is Agro Predict?
A decentralized prediction market for forecasting agricultural events using blockchain and oracles.

## 2. How do I participate?
Connect a wallet (MetaMask/binance wallet), stake APM tokens, and submit your prediction on a live market.

## 3. How are rewards calculated?
Rewards are proportional to prediction accuracy and stake amount.

## 4. Which data sources are used?
- Weather oracles  
- Commodity price feeds  
- Satellite and AI datasets

## 5. Is the platform secure?
Yes, contracts are designed for immutability, transparency, and security audits.

## 6. Can I suggest new markets?
Yes, community governance allows verified users to propose new markets.

## 7. How can I stay updated?
Follow official channels, check the web documentation, and subscribe to newsletters for updates.
